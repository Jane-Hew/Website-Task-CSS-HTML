Unit 2 1.5 Describe copyright and other constraints affecting websites

Copyright laws are put in place to protect intellectual properties on original works such as Photos, logos, ideas, music etc When creating a website you have to be very care what photos you are using. If you are using videos with music - do you have the right to use them.

Data protection Act impacts on how your site handles personal data. If you have an eCommerce business, you will need to be aware of this law. You will need to think how you are tracking customers, storing delivery addresses etc.

Unit 2 1.6 Describing access issues that need taking into account

People with disabilities face many issues when using the internet. This could be due to sight, hearing, language and other disabilities.

One simple thing that we can do is making sure we add a description for any images we might decide to put in our websites.

We need to think of the colours and Fonts that we are using. Certain colours can cause issues for people who are dyslexic for example.

Some of the big sites put things such as voice recognition into their sites for people who cannot spell or use a keyboard. They use readers for people who cannot see.

Innovation has seen things like Siri, Alexa, Bixby to name a few come forward which use software that recognises and learns speech. Machine learning allows them to even pick up in nuances from different dialects.
