Unit 1 3.1 Review the benefits and drawbacks of IT tools and systems used in terms of productivity and efficiency.

I feel the task could have run more efficiently if it was done as part of a team project. By using Trello you can break The project down into individual tasks that team members could have each done. This way you can work in a more Agile way, where team members play to their strengths, which helps with decision making and creation of project. The reviewing part could have been done by the team in general to come back with a more comprehensive review on coding, written work etc.

A drawback for me was that I am not very experienced using code and I am not a designer. Individual team members could have played to their strengths whilst doing the task, such as some people creating the HTML side of the code and some people working on the design using CSS.

Data protection Act impacts on how your site handles personal data. If you have an eCommerce business, you will need to be aware of this law. You will need to think how you are tracking customers, storing delivery addresses etc.

Unit 1 Outcome 3.3 Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects.

A way to improve productivity and efficiency for myself would be to ensure that I take regular breaks away from the screen. This is to allow my eyes to rest and freshen me up before I start again. When working from home in future I will ensure that I have a much better and more comfortable home office set up because at present my desk is at work top height and my chair has to be high to accommodate this, which means that my feet are off the floor and I have to have a foot rest.
